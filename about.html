How it all began

Between A block and the primary school was an overgrown section of land with beautiful sunlight bu not being used. The Earth & Environmental Science students were learning about climate change and wanted to create a sustainabilty project, which lead to the thought of a native food and medicine garden.

Where it is located

The gardenThe garden can be found on the northside of the school, just outside of A block.
Slightly hidden from the day-to-day school life the garden offers a tranquil setting for the plants to grow and students to visit.
So students can learn about the different native food and medicine plants two large sign display the map of the plant locations.

Garden used to build our understanding

Australian native plants have adapted to our dry climate and poor quality soil. Unfortunately the wonderful variety of flavours in our native plants are often not known or grown This garden aims to encourage others to discover the wonder variety of Australian native food and medicine plants.
